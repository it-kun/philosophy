Digital Modernism
Postmodernism has had a widespread impact across various fields in modern society since the mid-20th century.
It deconstructed traditional and fixed notions,
as well as absolute truths in literature, art, architecture, philosophy, etc.,
highlighting the importance of freedom and emotion in our lives.

>I wish to continue the vein of Postmodernism into what I call ' Digital Modernism. '
Digital Modernism explores the interaction between technology and art
and seeks new forms of creation and expression by reflecting social and cultural changes in the digital age.
